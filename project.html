Bagaicha Adventure Resort

Bagaicha Adventure Resort promotes cultural and adventure tourism while embracing the site’s natural setting, which includes fruit trees and landscaped gardens. The development maintains a symbiotic relationship with nature. Covering an area of 1.61 hectares, the site extends in a north–south direction. The planning approach emphasizes minimal intervention to existing site conditions, resulting in a layout where built structures merge seamlessly with the natural environment, similar to elements within a forest. The development consists of five main components: the entrance gate, arrival block, bar, banquet hall, and accommodation units. Each component is designed to blend with its surroundings while maintaining a clear functional distinction between spaces. The entrance gate serves as a landmark through its scale and ornamental features, while the arrival block represents hospitality by providing parking and essential services. As the site extends southward, it widens at the extremities, where an array of trees enhances the landscape and integrates harmoniously with the built structures.

WHAT - Rammed Earth Resort

WHERE - Gaidakot11, Harkapur, Nawalparasi

WHEN - March 2018- 2022

HimalayaAgro Cold Storage, Kagbeni

Designed to harness the natural thermal mass of rammed earth walls — 2,014 cubic feet of compacted material — for passive temperature regulation, preserving agricultural produce without mechanical refrigeration.
