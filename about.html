About Us

Pioneering Sustainable Built Environment in Nepal

Ramro Mato, meaning "Good Mud," is a private firm dedicated to sustainable built environment practices through design and empirical solutions. Formed in 2018, we are a multidisciplinary team of architects, engineers, and builders specialising in sustainable building design and construction.

Our vision — Earth Enriched Environment — is a state where earth ameliorates and the good earth paradigm provides for sustained well-being. We promote the application of different forms of earth as building construction material and actively participate in sustainable built environment practices across Nepal.

Design Approach

From predesign analysis through working drawings and permit documentation, we deliver fully integrated architectural design services tailored to sustainable rammed earth construction.

Innovative Solutions

We apply earthen building technologies — including PP bi-axial geogrid reinforcement, multi-color clay composites, and passive solar design — to create durable, climate-responsive structures.

Project Management

Our multidisciplinary team coordinates every stage — from site evaluation and soil testing to quality control and final handover — ensuring your project is delivered on time and to standard.
